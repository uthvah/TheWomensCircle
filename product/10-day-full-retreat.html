About this retreat

Welcome to RetreatYou, a sanctuary nestled in the serene mountains of Thailand, where transformative experiences await you. Whether you choose our 10-day Full Retreat, 5-day Yoga Retreat, or 7-day Yoga Programme, each offering promises to rejuvenate your mind, body, and spirit.

Blissful ambience of RetreatYou

Immerse yourself in the blissful ambiance of RetreatYou as you embark on a journey of self-discovery and holistic wellness. Our experienced instructors will guide you through invigorating yoga sessions, tailored to your skill level and designed to deepen your practice. Explore a variety of yoga styles, from gentle Hatha to dynamic Vinyasa flows, allowing you to find your own rhythm and reconnect with your inner self.

In addition to the enriching yoga sessions, our retreats offer a plethora of wellness activities to enhance your experience. Engage in guided meditation sessions that will bring tranquility to your mind and promote a sense of inner peace.

Discover the power of mindful eating through workshops led by our expert nutritionist, where you will learn how to nourish your body with wholesome, nutritious meals.

As you embrace the serene beauty of the Thai mountains, allow the surroundings to inspire and rejuvenate you. Take moments of solitude to immerse yourself in nature, breathing in the fresh mountain air and finding solace in the tranquil surroundings. Connect with like-minded individuals on this journey, forging lasting friendships and creating a supportive community.

Focus on what's important to you

Whether you join us for the full 10-day retreat, a shorter 5-day escape, or the focused 7-day yoga programme, RetreatYou offers a holistic experience that goes beyond yoga and wellness. It is a sanctuary where you can replenish your soul, find inner balance, and return to your daily life with a renewed sense of vitality and purpose.

Come and experience the transformative magic of RetreatYou, where every retreat is designed to nurture your mind, revitalize your body, and awaken your spirit. Embrace this opportunity to invest in your well-being and embark on a journey of self-discovery that will leave you feeling empowered and inspired.
