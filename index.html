Frequently asked questions

What is a Women's Circle?

A Women's Circle is a nurturing and empowering space where women gather to connect, share experiences, and support each other in both personal and professional growth.

What sets this Women's Circle apart?

It's not merely a one-time experience; it's about prioritizing self-care, making consistent progress, working on personal growth while supporting others, and evolving together.

Who is welcome to join the Women's Circle?

Our Women's Circle warmly welcomes women of all ages, backgrounds, and professions who share a passion for cultivating meaningful connections, building confidence, and contributing to a supportive community.

When does the Women's Circle typically happen?

We meet on alternate Fridays from 6:30 PM to 8:00 PM, with three terms a year and 6-8 sessions per term:
Term 1: Jan - Feb
Term 2: March - June
Term 3: Sept - Nov

Why create a Women's Circle?

This space empowers women, uniquely positioned, to recognize and unleash their impact. When happy, they hold the ability to influence many around them, creating a transformative ripple effect across their communities.

Is there a fee to be part of the Women's Circle community?

Yes, there is an affordable term fee based on the number of sessions.

How can I suggest topics or activities for future Women's Circle sessions?

We value your input! Share your ideas through our website, shaping the content of upcoming terms. This initiative is experimental, with no precedence, and your support is instrumental in charting its unique path.

Do Women's Circle events cater to introverts or those new to networking?

Absolutely. Our events are designed to be inclusive, creating a supportive environment for connections at your own pace.

How can I stay informed about upcoming Women's Circle events?

Stay updated by joining the WhatsApp group/events page on the website for the latest updates and event announcements aligning with our mission.

How does the Women's Circle contribute to a positive impact on families and communities?

Through our mission-driven activities, we aim to extend a positive impact by empowering women, fostering connections, and creating a ripple effect of support and celebration that reaches families and communities.

I have more questions. How can I get in touch?

Feel free to reach out through our company email (thewomenscirclehub@gmail.com). We're eager to assist and answer any additional questions you may have about our circle and its mission.
